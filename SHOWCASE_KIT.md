## 5. Student Showcase Pitch

Short pitch:

`PantryPal helps you figure out what to cook using ingredients you already have at home. Instead of searching for perfect recipes, it gives you realistic meal ideas, simple steps, and a tiny grocery list if you're missing one or two things.`

Slightly stronger pitch:

`PantryPal is an AI-powered meal planning app designed to reduce food waste and decision fatigue. You enter the ingredients you already have, and it generates simple, realistic meal ideas that work for busy people, low-energy nights, and limited pantry situations.`
